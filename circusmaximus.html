Circus Maximus Diorama

3D-printed interactive diorama representing the Circus Maximus - an Ancient Roman Chariot Racing stadium active between ~500BCE till ~550AD.

Turning a crank brings the chariot to life - allowing the chariots within to move along a predetermined path.

At its peak during the height of the Roman Empire, the Circus Maximus could seat over 100,000 people - almost a tenth of the total population of Rome.
